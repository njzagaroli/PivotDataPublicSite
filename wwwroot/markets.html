PivotData has developed an Investment Data Warehouse with special focus on portfolio management and private funds to provide a single data repository, a defined corporate data model with a single set of rules and a one-stop-shop for definitions and data utilization across the organization.

PivotData’s data warehouse is a Microsoft SQL based Investment Data Warehouse solution created with client input to address the design considerations of IBOR (Investment Book of Records), data governance, data process, and data quality management. Our approach is position-centric while addressing the organizations need for access to transactional data. The central requirement of our data warehouse design is to deliver timely and accurate data across the enterprise, fulfilling a wide range of business needs:

  • The ability to access timely, accurate, and consolidated data across the enterprise
  • The ability to measure objectively the quality of data
  • The ability to control the data management process through meaningful reconciliations
  • The ability to establish standards across the organization for the publication and utilization of data
  • The ability to respond to the ever-increasing data needs of clients and regulators
  • The ability to reduce and manage the risk associated with data governance
The PivotData system is a purpose-built system for the investment industry, with a focus on portfolio management and private funds, incorporating extensive input and field testing from customer with our knowledge and expertise in the data management business.
