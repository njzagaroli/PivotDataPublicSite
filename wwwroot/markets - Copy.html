PivotData’s has developed an Investment Data Warehouse for the investment industry allowing clients to deploy a market ready data warehouse which incorporates all of their vendor data and provides access to data for reporting and analytics needs.

PivotData’s data warehouse is a Microsoft SQL based Investment Data Warehouse solution created with client input to address the design considerations of IBOR (Investment Book of Records) data management. As such our approach is position centric while addressing the needs for access to transactional data. The central requirement of our data warehouse design is to deliver data which is timely and accurate. The ultimate goal is to support a range of business needs, including but not limited to:

  • The need for investment decision makers to base their investment decision making on timely and accurate data
  • The need to be able to measure objectively the quality of data and to control this through meaningful reconciliations
  • The need for data used in client reporting and performance to be consistent with the data used in the management of assets.

In addition to these primary data needs driven from the business, there is a key supporting objective of data quality management.
