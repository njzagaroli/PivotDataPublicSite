Our Focus

At PivotData we are focused on designing, developing and implementing efficient data warehouse solutions for the investment industry. We are differentiated by our unwavering client focus and the ability to solve complex data organizational issues.

Over the years our consultants have completed data warehousing projects for some of the largest and most reputable investment firms in the country. Our team of engineers and consultants possess deep industry knowledge in the support of Alternative Investments Firms, Single and Multi Family Offices, Fund of Funds, Insurance Companies and Pension Funds.
