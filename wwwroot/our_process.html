The PivotData data warehouse is designed to deliver on two fundamental principles: Data Governance and support for the key data structures (“Data Structure Widgets”) for investment managers. Our goals were to keep the system simple, cost effective, and flexible for rapid deployment. Our mission is to provide your organization with real time information regarding quality of your organizations data, including the who, what, where, and when of your data.


Data governance: The PivotData implementation is a defined process an organization follows to ensure that high quality data exists throughout the complete lifecycle. The key focus areas of data governance include availability, usability, integrity and security. This includes establishing processes to ensure important data assets are formally managed throughout the enterprise, and the data can be trusted for decision-making. Often the processes used in data governance include accountability for any adverse event that results from data quality. When companies desire, or are required, to gain control of their data, they empower their people, set up processes and get help from technology to do it.

Data Structure Widgets: The PivotData Data Structure Widgets are a collection of plug-in modules to kick-start the collection and management of your organization's metadata. We have created a set of standard operational data stores to manage the data structures commonly found within the back-office management of the investment management firm. Defined Data Structure Widgets allow your organization to kick start the collection and documentation of your data’s “what” (i.e., Accounts, Entities, Positions, Investments, Transactions, etc.).
