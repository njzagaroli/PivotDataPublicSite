LP and Allocator Cash Flow Forecasting

PivotData’s Cash Flow Forecasting Application provides LP’s and Allocators with the ability to manage and project cash flows for all types of investments, including alternative investments such as private equity and real estate transactions. Our cash flow Application will enable you to model current and future investments, taking into account different events such as changes in drawdowns and distributions, multiple hurdle rates, and changes in EBITDA forecast, and apply those changes to entire Portfolios, individual Funds, Deals or Positions.

PivotData’s Cash Flow Forecasting Application allows LP’s to automate waterfall calculation processes and scenario forecasting capabilities. Coupled with our data warehouse solution, our Cash Flow Forecasting Application will allow you to report cash flow forecasts for any Investment, Entity or ownership structure.

Benefits of PivotData’s Cash Flow Forecasting Application

  • Integrated into our Data Warehouse with intuitive input sheets allowing users to input and update Fund, Deal and Position data.
  • Integrated cashflow reporting including detailed Drawdown and Distribution schedules, multiple Hurdle Rate Waterfall tables and NAV tables at the FUND or Deal level.
  • Built-in functions allow for re-forecasting of cash flows based cashflow Scenarios at the Fund or Portfolio level
  • Reforecasting of cashflow based changes in IRR or expected multiples and changes in Portfolio Company EBITDA.
  • Includes cash flow forecasting report for any entity or group of entities.

LP and Allocators – ILPA Reporting

ILPA’s Capital Call / Distribution Template
PivotData has integrated ILPA’s capital call and distribution recommendations into our ETL and data warehouse process to facilitate the collection and processing of capital call information between the LP and GP. Our incorporation of the ILPA reporting guidelines will serve several purposes, including, but not limited to:

  • Enabling the LP to interpret and account for a transaction accurately
  • Reducing LP and GP processing times and ultimately reducing monitoring costs
  • Improving LP-GP communications in regards to an investment’s status

The Capital Call and Distribution Notice data is automatically uploaded to the data warehouse, and updates the relevant financial data and cash flow forecast as required.
