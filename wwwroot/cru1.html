Overview

Overview

In today's complex regulatory environment, data management has emerged as one of the most challenging aspects of compliance reporting and internal risk management. Private fund managers have to invest increasing amounts of time to collate, store, extract and compute information from more data sources than ever before. In addition, advisers need to be prepared for possible changes in reporting requirements. The ability to quickly and efficiently extract and analyze information for multiple purposes or more critical than ever before.

Introduction to PivotData's CRU Solution

PivotData's CRU (Compliance Reporting Utility) provides the alternative investment adviser community the ability to translate data into categories and schema required to meet today's internal and external reporting requirements. CRU gives the knowledgeable users (not the IT department) control over the data loading and normalization process for each of their data sources in a secure, simple, transparent and cost effective manner and is designed to adapt to future requirements as well. CRU provides advisers with a single data depository that can be utilized to generate regulatory compliance, management, investor and marketing reports. The approach reduces risk, ensures consistency, saves time and ensures that reporting needs are in line with the same CFO and CCO approved underlying data sets.

Business Benefits

  • Minimized business-IT divide for improved reaction time
  • Increased efficiency in reporting and data gathering for tangible business decisions
  • Enhanced data integrity and control
  • Mitigated data and process risk
