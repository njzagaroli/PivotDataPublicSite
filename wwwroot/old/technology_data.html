Investment Data Warehouse

PivotData’s data warehouse is a pre-configured and customizable investment data warehouse based on the Microsoft SQL Server platform. We specialize in developing and implementing data warehouses for the investment industry. Our team fully understands the unique reporting and analytical requirements and the complex ownership structures of each type of entity. Our consultants will lead your team through the process of needs assessment, validation of key measures and the development of your organizations data model.

PivotData’s Data Warehouse serves as a single, central repository for your company’s investment holdings (positions and transactions), reference data, performance data, regulatory and compliance data and CRM data. PivotData’s data model was developed over time and has been deployed at leading investment management firms. The data model supports a wide spectrum of asset classes from simple fixed income and equity to complex, derivatives structured products and alternatives. The data model contains pre-defined modules and dimensions for data from your general ledger, payables, receivables, projects, fixed assets and more, as well as user-defined modules for any other data source and dimensions you need for your data warehouse.

PivotData’s data warehouse is a historical data repository which will deliver consistent reporting and analytics across the organization and at any entity level required. The data warehouse is built on Microsoft SQL technology and is a scalable data warehouse capable of handling high volumes of data.
