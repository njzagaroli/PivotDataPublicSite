The PivotData data warehouse is a system comprised of modules designed to provide systems process support, ETL (extract, transform, & load) processes, and Data Structure Widgets. We have created a solution that combines our three decades on experience and knowledge of data management with over a decade of experience addressing the unique challenges facing the traditional investment management firm’s data needs.

The Modules
Process   Manages the scheduling and data dependencies, when and in what order the data sources are processed
Data  Manages the metadata, describes the objects in your system, data sources, tables, attributes, calculations, transformation, and rules
Batch  Manages the processes for transforming the source data to published
Notification  Monitors the processing and data quality, provides real time status of for each step of the process, alerting IT when issues are detected and informing the business of the status of your data
Publish   Manages the creation, distribution, and publishing the subject data marts

We set out to create a solution that was economical and could be implemented rapidly. Our solution is designed to seamlessly integrate with your business, we don’t change the way or how you work; we consolidate and streamline your efforts to collect, transform, format, and publish data, reducing risk and errors, while improving quality and access to data.
The Process
Describe the data
Map the values to one our Data Structure Widgets or define your own
Apply prebuilt rules to the data or define your one
Publish the data
